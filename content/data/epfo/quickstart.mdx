## EPFO

### What is EPFO?

The Employees’ Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO) is a statutory body under the Ministry of Labour and Employment, Government of India. It regulates and manages provident funds, pensions, and insurance schemes for employees across India. 

EPFO also oversees social security agreements with countries that have bilateral arrangements with India, ensuring international workers are covered under the EPFO plans.

Read more about EPFO and the various documents supported <a href="https://www.epfindia.gov.in/site_en/index.php" target="blank">here</a>.

<hr className="primary" />

### What can I do with this product?

Fetch the Universal Account Number (UAN) of a user by providing their contact number.

Fetch the user's personal and employment details by providing the UAN number.

This API documentation provides details for integrating UAN-related functionalities into your application, enabling efficient management of employee provident fund details.


Here’s a quick overview of the EPFO API. Additionally, here are the URLs you would need for this API—

- Sandbox—`https://dg-sandbox.setu.co`
- Production—`https://dg.setu.co`
- Headers—Contact Setu for providing the credentials required to successfully call Setu APIs. This contains:
  - `x-client-id`
  - `x-client-secret`
  - `x-product-instance-id`

### UAN Lookup

Call this API to fetch the user's UAN. Pass the below parameter in the request—

- `mobileNumber` is the user's mobile number. This should be registered with the EPFO.
