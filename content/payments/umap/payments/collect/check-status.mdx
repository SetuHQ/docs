## Check collect request payment status

This is a generic payment status api, that accepts the id of the collect request to show the status of the last payment.

  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <th>Staging host</th>
      <td>

      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <th>Method</th>
      <td>
        <code>GET</code>
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <th>Path</th>
      <td>
        <code>
          /v1/merchants/payments/product-instances/&#123;collectRequestId&#125;/last

      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
