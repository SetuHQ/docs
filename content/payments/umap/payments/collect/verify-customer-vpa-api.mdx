## Verify customer VPA

<p>
  This gives a merchant an information about the{" "}
  <b>
    <i>customer vpa, payment mode</i>
  </b>{" "}
  which helps the merchant to decide whether to initiate the collect request or
  not.
</p>

<table>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <th>Staging host</th>
      <td>
        <code>https://umap.setu.co/api</code>
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <th>Method</th>
      <td>
        <code>POST</code>
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <th>Path</th>
      <td>
        <code>/v1/merchants/customer-vpas/verify</code>
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <th>Header</th>
      <td>
        <code>merchantId</code>
        <br />
        <code>Authorization</code>: Bearer &lt;<b>insert_token_here</b>&gt;.
        Generate this token using{" "}
        <a href="/payments/upi-deeplinks/resources/oauth" target="_blank">
          OAuth
        </a>{" "}
        or{" "}
        <a href="/payments/upi-deeplinks/resources/jwt" target="_blank">
          JWT
        </a>
        .<br />
        <br />
        <code>Content-Type</code>—will be <code>application/json</code>
      </td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <th>API reference</th>
      <td>
        <a
          href="/payments/umap/api-reference#/category~customer-vpa/operation~customer-vpa#verify"
          target="_blank"
        >
          Verify customer VPA ↗
        </a>
      </td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

###### Sample request

<CodeBlockWithCopy language="json">
  {`{
    "customerVpa": "dekup@pineaxis",
    "merchantReferenceId": "1240689979139556557"
}`}
</CodeBlockWithCopy>

###### Sample response

<CodeBlockWithCopy language="json">
  {`{
  "createdAt": "2023-09-04 12:08:20.202644 +0530 IST",
  "customerName": "deku",
  "customerVpa": "ram@okhdfc",
  "id": "01ARZ3NDEKTSV4RRFFQ69G5FAV",
  "merchantId": "01ARZ3NDEKTSV4RRFFQ69G5FAV",
  "merchantReferenceId": "1240689979139556557",
  "status": "pending"
}`}
</CodeBlockWithCopy>
