## Overview

### What is the UPI Setu?

UPI Setu is a comprehensive UPI product suite designed by Setu to empower **_merchants_** and **_merchant aggregators_** by providing state-of-the-art UPI solutions.

UPI Setu connects directly with NPCI, absorbing both acquirer and beneficiary bank sides of the transaction to provide a seamless UPI-first product experience to our customers.

_<Text size="small">Unified Payments Interface (UPI) is a digital payment system developed by NPCI that enables customers to pay for goods and services real-time, across any UPI app with different payment methods—like savings accounts or credit cards—from the convenience of their phone.</Text>_

#### Key features

- **Express ledger**—Fully managed beneficiary leg to drastically reduce deemed transactions and avoid bank failures.
- **Refunds and disputes**—Specialised APIs to handle refunds and disputes, thus increasing operational value.
- **Latest NPCI compliant stack**—Adherence to the latest UPI offerings and compliances, because of the direct partnership with NPCI.

<br />

#### What can you do with UPI Setu?

- **One time UPI payments**—Enable all UPI payments using QR, Payment Links and in-app deployment with our APIs.
- **Recurring mandate payments**—Customisable recurring payments with UPI Mandates for all your use cases.
- **UPI for financial services**—Enable BFSI specific products like Third Party Validation and Single Block Multi Debit.
