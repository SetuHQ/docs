## Notifications and alerts for customer VPA verification

These statuses are relevant for the VPA verification done under the <a href="/payments/umap/payments/collect" target="_self">UPI payments collect request</a> product.

### Details for customer VPA verification statuses

<Text>
  <ul>
    <li>
      <code>customer_vpa.valid</code>&nbsp;Provided VPA is valid and associated
      with a bank account
    </li>
    <li>
      <code>customer_vpa.invalid</code>&nbsp;Provided VPA is invalid and account
      details could not be fetched
    </li>
    <li>
      <code>customer_vpa.failed</code>&nbsp;Failed to fetch VPA details
    </li>
  </ul>
</Text>
