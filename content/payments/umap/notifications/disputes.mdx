### Details for dispute statuses
The UMAP platform's seamless integration with the UDIR framework ensures that merchants can provide prompt and efficient resolutions to disputes, safeguarding both their interests and those of their customers. 
<img
    src="https://storage.googleapis.com/strapi-assets/latest/Frame_73/Frame_73.png"

The statuses are consistent across all UMAP productsâ€”
<Text>
    <ul>
        <li><code>Dispute.open</code>&nbsp;A dispute has been raised by a customer, against a payment they did before</li>
        <li><code>Dispute.closed</code>&nbsp;The merchant has accepted the dispute claim from customer, and issued a refund</li>
        <li><code>Dispute.in-review</code>&nbsp;The merchant has contested the dispute claim from customer, and provided evidence of goods/service delivery</li>
        <li><code>Dispute.won</code>&nbsp;Merchant evidence is accepted and no refund is required</li>
        <li><code>Dispute.lost</code>&nbsp;Merchant evidence is rejected and a refund is issued automatically</li>
    </ul>
</Text>
